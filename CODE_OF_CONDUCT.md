# Code of Conduct

KICKGEIST is for fans. This community — players, contributors, and maintainers —
should feel as welcoming as watching a match with friends.

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation a friendly, harassment-free experience for
everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity
and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race,
religion, or which team you support.

## Our standards

Things that keep this community great:

- Be welcoming and patient, especially with newcomers and first-time contributors.
- Be respectful of differing viewpoints, club allegiances, and experiences.
- Give and accept constructive feedback gracefully.
- Show empathy toward other community members.
- Keep it about football and fair play.

Things that are not OK:

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments of any kind.
- Discriminatory language or imagery, and unwelcome attention.
- Publishing others' private information without permission.
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a friendly, public setting.

## Scope

This applies in all project spaces (issues, pull requests, discussions) and when
someone is representing the project in public spaces.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please open a confidential
report via the repository's security/contact channel, or email the maintainers.
All reports are reviewed and handled with discretion.

## Enforcement

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying these standards and may take fair,
appropriate action in response to behavior they deem inappropriate — from a
gentle reminder to removing contributions or participation rights for repeated
or serious violations.

## Attribution

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the spirit of the Contributor Covenant,
tailored for the KICKGEIST community.
